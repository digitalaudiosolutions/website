THE digitalEQ

The digitalEQ introduces the ability to equalize music to your audio stack, a feature that is commonly forgotten or poorly implemented when moving away from all in one systems.

autorenew

EQ your sound with a mix of old and new

The box will contain the familiar ability to EQ your music with settings like rock and pop, adjusting the bass, mid, and treb, and saving a couple of custom settings. Unique to our box, are some less common EQ settings that you wouldn't typically find.

Keep the integrity of your sound

The digitalEQ works strickly with digital audio interfaces, ensuring the integrity of the digital input signal and allowing easy integration into an audio stack before the DAC.
